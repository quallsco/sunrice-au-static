RICE SIDE BABY

Vegetable Rice Side

5 min prep
15 min cook
20 min total
Serves 5

Ingredients

  • 1 tbsp Olive Oil
  • 1 Onion diced
  • 3 clove Garlic crushed
  • 2-3 cup Birds Eye Frozen Veggie Mix of your choice
  • 4 cup Vegetable Stock

Rice

2 cup

SunRice Basmati Rice

uncooked (If using Basmati, rinse rice 3-4 times to remove starch)

Shop this rice

STEP 1 - PREP

  • Cook onion and garlic in olive oil for 1-2 minutes in pot over medium to high heat
  • Add vegetables and stir for 2-3 minutes

STEP 2 - COOK

  • Add in the rinsed rice & stock and cover
  • Cook until liquid has been absorbed

STEP 3 - SERVE

  • Once liquid is absorbed, fluff up the rice with a fork
  • Serve with protein of your choice (try Steak)

PRO TIP - STORE

  • Store your left over rice in a sealable, microwave-friendly tub for another day.

Make it Vego

Portobello Mushrooms - Substitute for steak
