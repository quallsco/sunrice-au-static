SunRice Recipe

Orange Satay Salmon Rice Bowl in 5

5 min prep
5 min cook
10 min total

Adjust recipe

Servings

Originally serves 2. Scaled quantities are approximate.

Ingredients

  • 1 packet SunRice Microwave Jasmine Rice
  • 0.25 cup Satay Sauce
  • 1 orange
  • 400 g Smoked Salmon
  • 2 cup Packet Cabbage Slaw
  • 4 tbsp Peanuts crushed

Method

1 Heat SunRice Microwave Jasmine Rice in the microwave for 90sec. Then place it in a large serving bowl.
2 Cut orange into quarters and squeeze the juice of one quarter into a small bowl. Add the satay sauce into the bowl with the orange juice and stir to combine.
3 Add the cabbage slaw to one side of the bowl with the rice, then place your cut of salmon on top.
4 Drizzle over the orange satay sauce and top with the crushed peanuts and a few pieces of orange and enjoy.
