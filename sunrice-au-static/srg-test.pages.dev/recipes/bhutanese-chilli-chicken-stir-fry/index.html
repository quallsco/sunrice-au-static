Bhutanese Chilli Chicken Stir Fry

Enjoy Asian cuisine at home with this Bhutanese chilli chicken stir fry! This dish is full of flavour and tasty ingredients, perfect for lunch or dinner.

10 min prep
30 min cook
40 min total

Rice used

Featured product

Microwave Rice

SunRice Microwave Black Rice Pouch

This quick and easy wholegrain rice is deliciously nutty and a vibrant purple colour ready in just 90 seconds.

Available sizes

250g pouch
View more

Adjust recipe

Servings

Originally serves 5. Scaled quantities are approximate.

Ingredients

  • 0.67 cup SunRice Black Rice
  • 280 g Chicken Thigh Fillets thickly sliced
  • 0.25 cup Cornflour
  • 0.67 tbsp Vegetable Oil
  • 1.25 tsp Sesame Oil
  • 0.33 Brown Onion cut into thin wedges
  • 1.25 Long Red Chillies seeded and finely chopped
  • 0.67 tbsp Ginger finely grated
  • 1.25 clove Garlic crushed
  • 80 g Mixed Asian Mushrooms sliced (separate enoki, if using)
  • 0.33 bunch Gai Lan coarsely chopped (separate stems from leaves)
  • 0.75 Tomatoes coarsely chopped
  • cup Soy Sauce
  • 0.75 tbsp Water

Method

1 Cook rice according to directions on pack.
2 Place chicken and cornflour in a snap lock or freezer bag and shake, to coat.
3 Heat a wok over a high heat. Add half the vegetable oil and swirl to coat. Add half the chicken and stir-fry for about 5 minutes until golden brown. Remove chicken and set aside. Repeat with remaining vegetable oil and chicken.
4 Reduce heat to medium. Add sesame oil and onions to wok. Stir-fry 1 minute or until onions begin to soften. Add chillies, ginger and garlic and stir-fry for 1 minute or until fragrant. Return chicken to wok, along with mushrooms and gai lan stems, leaving out any delicate mushrooms such as enoki. Stir-fry for 5 minutes or until mushrooms are tender and chicken is cooked through.
5 Add gai lan leaves, tomatoes, soy sauce, water and any reserved mushrooms. Stir-fry for 1-2 minutes or until gai lan has wilted.
6 Serve over rice.
